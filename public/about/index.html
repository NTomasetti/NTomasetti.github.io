About

Hi, I’m a PhD Candidate with the Department of Econometrics and Business Statistics in Monash Univerisity. I’m working with Catherine Forbes and Anastasios Panagiotelis to develop fast algorithms to update forecasts as more data is observed. For more details, see my research page.

I’m also teaching Statistical Methods for Insurance, and Financial Econometrics at Monash, and I’ll add more details for these subjects throughout the next two semesters as the classes progress.

I work mostly with R and C++, but I’ve thrown a bit of Edward (python) and Stan in as well.
